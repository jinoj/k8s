Pods States:
------------
				Pending   : in progress
				Running
				Succeeded : successfully exited
				Failed
				Unknown

Launching Pods
--------------
# kubectl apply -f [pod filename].yam;

Monitering all resorces
-----------------------
# watch -n 1  kubectl get pods,deploy,rs,svc

View Pods
----------
#kubectl get pods
#kubectl get po -o wide
#kubectl get pods [Pod Name]

Pods Details information:
-------------------------
#kubectl describe pods [Pod Name]


Operate the pod
---------------
#kubectl logs [Pod Name]
#kubectl exec -it [Pode Name] bash   #Username with Hostname
#kubectl exec -it [Pode Name] sh     #Hostname

MultiContainer Pods
--------------------
#kubectl logs [Pod Name] -c [Container Name]
#kubectl exec -it [Pode Name] -c [Container Name] bash   #Username with Hostname
#kubectl exec -it [Pode Name] -c [Container Name] sh     #Hostname
